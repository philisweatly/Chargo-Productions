Our Mission

To bring home owners together with the people who make their homes feel like...home

  • What We Do

    The Santa Barbara and Central Coast Home Shows showcase over 100 Exhibitors and Sponsors offering expert ideas and products in all facets of indoor and outdoor home improvements.

  • Our Vendors

    Each show brings us a huge diversity in vendors to meet all of your home design needs. From landscaping to roofing to that above ground jacuzzi you always wants. Our home shows have it all.

  • Our Shows

    We have shows 4 times a year in two locations. Santa Barabara, CA at the Earl Warren Showgrounds as well as Santa Maria at the Whatever Showgrounds.
